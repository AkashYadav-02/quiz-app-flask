Why Quizes are Important


Quizzes are good for revision Pre-test quizzes can help pupils revise without even realising they are doing it. And a quiz is a good way for a pupil to identify and take ownership of their knowledge gaps. It's also a great way to help them to plug the gaps to boost their confidence when it comes to formal testing
